International Mathematics Research Society

The International Mathematics Research Society (IMRS) aims to foster global collaboration among mathematicians, researchers, and scholars by promoting the advancement of mathematical knowledge through high-quality research, discussions, and innovations.

Our objective is to provide a platform for the exchange of ideas, dissemination of cutting-edge mathematical discoveries, and encouragement of interdisciplinary applications of mathematics in science, engineering, and technology.
